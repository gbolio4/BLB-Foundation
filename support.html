Lifting Destinies, Our Passion

Help us execute our life-changing programmes by supporting our work, which depends heavily on the freewill donations of good people like you.  

Donate Cash Give Items

Your support, however little,  will help save/restore destinies of some of the most socially neglected, underrepresented and unprivileged members of our local communities.

More than 95%  or more of all donations that the BLB Foundation recieves go straight to those in need through a plethora of social projects, such as education, food and cloths distribution to the poor, water, shelters, and other types of projects or programmes that can help empower the indigent especially women, youth and children.  We are an ethical organization; our only purpose is to help empower, uplift and to give voice to the needy and disadvantaged, children inclusive.

With your donation, you will be helping the dissillusioned and the despondent community members receive a chance at meaningful survival,  including helping kids get fair and affordable education, something many of us have taken for granted.

When you donate to BLB Foundation, rest assured that you are helping to provide the hope of a better future to people in dire need. There are a few ways to get involved in our work to reduce poverty and provide life-changing help to our focus group. However, the easiest way is to make a cash donation using our safe and secure payment processor,  with a debit or a credit card.
