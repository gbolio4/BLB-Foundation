MISSION

To empower, uplift and positively change the life outcomes of the poor and neglected in our local communities in  active partnership with like-minded organizations, individuals and governments.


VISION

To make a recognizable and real enough impact with our work such that our impact grants us recognition to be reckoned as one of the world's top 10 non-profits in our area of focus.
